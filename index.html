About Me

Hi, I'm Golden. Honestly? I love figuring things out and offering solutions.

For years, I did that in customer service, helping people untangle difficult situations by listening closely, breaking problems down, and finding solutions that actually worked. I'm motivated by solving real problems and seeing solutions that work.

I made the move to transition into tech because I want to keep solving real-world problems through code.

At IT Careers, I've built and refined my frontend skillset with React, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, and I'm now deepening my backend knowledge to grow into a developer who can confidently build across multiple layers of the stack.

My customer service background has given me valuable transferable skills that I bring to software development. I get to the root of the issue, solve it cleanly, and make the experience better than expected.

When I'm not coding, I'm usually unwinding with a great comedy movie or getting stuck into something creative.
